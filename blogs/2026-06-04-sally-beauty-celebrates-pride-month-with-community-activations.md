Sally Beauty is marking Pride Month with community-focused events in Los Angeles and New York City as part of its "Rooted in Success" initiative. The company will distribute complimentary products and gift cards at the WeHo Pride Parade on June 7 and host an interactive experience at NYC Pride on June 28. The retailer is also spotlighting LGBTQIA+ owned and founded beauty brands including Good Dye Young, Madison Reed, and XMONDO as part of its commitment to celebrating self-expression and inclusivity.
